# Romi Robots

Full Romi tutorials have not been created yet, but they are very similar to XRP robots.  The difference is the specific hardware you might use in your code.  Romi specific documentation can be found here.

* [WPILib Romi Classes](https://github.wpilib.org/allwpilib/docs/release/java/edu/wpi/first/wpilibj/romi/package-summary.html) - `RomiMotor`, `RomiGyro`&#x20;
* [Port numbers for Romi hardware](https://docs.wpilib.org/en/stable/docs/romi-robot/getting-to-know-romi.html)
* [Other hardware and sensors the Romi robot is compatible with from WPILib](https://docs.wpilib.org/en/stable/docs/romi-robot/hardware-support.html)
